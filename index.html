Refinishing Services

Your trusted specialists in refinishing, reglazing and resurfacing for bathtubs, showers, tile, countertops, cabinets, sinks and grout. Every surface gets expert care, from porcelain and fiberglass to cast iron, acrylic, ceramic and cultured marble, restored to a flawless, long-lasting finish.

Bathtub Refinishing

Our most popular service — restore fiberglass, steel, porcelain, or enamel bathtubs to brand-new condition in just a few hours using our high-quality Acrylic Polyurethane Enamel coating.
