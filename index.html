Our Services

Our Services

We refinish bathtubs, showers, tile, countertops, cabinets, sinks, grout and more — porcelain, fiberglass, cast iron, acrylic, ceramic tile and cultured marble.

Most Popular

Bathtub Refinishing

Restore fiberglass, steel, porcelain, or enamel bathtubs to brand-new condition in just a few hours using our high-quality Acrylic Polyurethane Enamel coating.

Shower Refinishing

Revitalize stained, chipped, and hard-to-clean shower surfaces. Our durable finish meets or exceeds manufacturer specs for stain and acid resistance.

Tile Refinishing

Re-glaze ceramic and porcelain tile without tearing out walls. We also restore grout for a complete, like-new bathroom surface.

Countertop Refinishing

Multi-Stone coatings give countertops a stone-type finish with a huge variety of colors — an affordable alternative to replacement.

Cabinet Refinishing

Refresh bathroom and kitchen cabinets without the cost of full replacement. Restore beauty and luster to worn cabinetry.

Sink Refinishing

Restore porcelain, fiberglass, and cultured marble sinks. Repair chips, holes, cracks, and rust — all surface types welcome.
